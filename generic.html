Advanced Functions in Kotlin

This content covers filter, map, let, also, apply and with. These functions occupy an important place in the industry and their use is crucial for speed, readability and functionality. These concepts are also known as Collection elements in Kotlin. These are also called scope functions. These functions can be used when dealing with objects and exchanging data via API. They are convenient for use in nullable expressions.
